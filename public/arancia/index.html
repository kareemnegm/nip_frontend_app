    THE ESSENCE OF
    CULTIVATED LIVING

    Arancia Yards is the first development to take shape within The Yards — BEYOND's landscape-led masterplan in City of Arabia, Dubai. The name arancia, Italian for orange, evokes the warmth, energy and richness of life lived fully. This character runs through every element of the project, from the facade composition to the communal gardens.

    Composed of three low-rise buildings arranged around a central green valley, Arancia Yards delivers 272 residences that prioritise space, light and connection to nature within one of Dubai's most strategically connected addresses.

    A slower rhythm unfolds beneath shaded avenues, fragrant orchards and garden-lined paths. Residents can wander to lively cafés, gather in vibrant plazas, enjoy moments of wellness, or explore the sports hub and community farm. Essential amenities including a nursery, school, clinic and mosque are seamlessly woven into the community, ensuring everyday needs are always close to home.

    OVERVIEW

    Landscape-Led Design
